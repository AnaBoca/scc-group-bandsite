THE BAND

Who is Cadillac Assembly Line? What is Cadillac Assembly Line? This five-piece joins those gritty, pleading vocals with the crisp notes of the guitar. Think rusted grill of an old caddy and the utter smoothness of Albert King's riffs. Cadillac Assembly Line transports audience members to a milieu of blues itself. If you're looking for some classic head-wigglin' jams across the Valley, check out the show dates below.

Doug Sawyer | Bass

Doug Sawyer started playing the bass professionally at age 14 when he joined The New Relations with current Branston star Doug Gabriel. Doug later played with The Silver Dollar Band with current international blues guitarist Craig Erickson. In 1992, Doug joined the popular Phoenix blues group, The Lynwood Flyers. He left The Flyers in 2005 and played regularly at church and irregularly throughout Phoenix in big bands and in pit orchestras. Doug played locally and on the road in New Mexico, Louisiana, and Texas with Aaron Thompson from 2005 to 2008. During the daylight hours, Doug is a chemistry professor at Scottsdale Community College.

Jim Elam | Guitar

Jim Elam starting playing guitar at age 15. He studied classical guitar in North Carolina and at Arizona State University where he received his Master's degree in music. After graduating, Jim switched to electric guitar and played for 12 years with the blues/soul band The Lynwood Flyers. Jim teaches computer classes at Scottsdale Community College.
